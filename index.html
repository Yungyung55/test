About the Summit

Gangnam Global Innovation Summit 2025 will bring together global IT professionals, startups, investors, and innovators to explore the latest trends in technology and foster new partnerships in the heart of Seoul's business hub.

Event Purpose

The summit aims to establish Gangnam as a global hub for IT innovation and startup growth, providing opportunities for networking, knowledge sharing, and investment.

Key Features

  • Keynote speeches from industry leaders
  • Panel discussions on startup ecosystem and investment strategies
  • Networking sessions with investors and startups
  • Interactive exhibitions showcasing innovative products and services
